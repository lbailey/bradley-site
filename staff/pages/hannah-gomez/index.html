Hannah

Gomez

Design Intern

Bio

Hannah is a Landscape Architecture student entering her fifth year at Penn State. All her life, she’s had a passion for art and for environmental science, and found a perfect union of both within the field of Landscape Architecture. Previously, Hannah taught art classes at Penn State and has interned with University Park’s Campus Planning and Design department. She has studied in Spain, New Zealand, and Australia, and she will spend her upcoming semester in Bonn, Germany. Hannah’s interests are in hand-graphics, in history, and in social and environmental justice.

Education

Hannah is working towards her Bachelor of Landscape Architecture (2020) at The Pennsylvania State University. She is a student in the Schreyer Honors College and was a 2019 recipient of the ASLA Merit Award.

Interests

Hiking, books, travel, any type of art-making, museums, parks, a good cup of coffee, sitting next to campfires, music, sunset and sunrise watching

Fun Fact

The strangest food Hannah has ever tried was a sweet pickle pie at a road-side diner in Utah, which she would recommend to anybody.
