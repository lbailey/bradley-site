Bel

St. John Day

Design Director | PM

Bio

Bel has ten years of experience with more than four years now at Bradley Site Design. Her experience ranges from US Embassy projects across the globe to in-fill redevelopments in Washington, DC. Bel provides design input on all projects within BSD and serves as project manager for all DC and Virginia projects and those outside of the DC metropolitan area. She loves the challenge(s) each new project brings and provides a holistic approach that addresses them with innovative solutions often pushing the boundaries of current sustainable design practices.
