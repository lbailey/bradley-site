Master Planning

Master Planning is a process of collaboration and discovery between stakeholders and experts that results in a plan that celebrates the culture in the built environment. The BSD team is a highly collaborative group of planners and landscape architects, committed to the development of sustainable cities that reflect the unique identity and culture of each community. Working with a wide network of multidisciplinary professionals, the team gathers information about the environmental, cultural and historic context of the project. Building on that foundation with the programmatic, financial and sustainability goals of the client, the design team applies its extensive knowledge of innovative planning concepts like New Urbanism and Smart Growth to produce a multifaceted and forward thinking Master Plan.
