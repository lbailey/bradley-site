The visual identity, the character and quality of life of a community is defined in large by its public spaces. Bradley Site Design provides place making and urban design services for projects ranging from intimate courtyard spaces to dynamic public plazas, streetscapes and expansive parks. Our clients are governments, developers, property management companies and community groups. Through a collaborative process the BSD team identifies the values, goals, and essential character of the place, then develops programs and plans that celebrate the unique identity of the community. We create vibrant town centers, solemn memorial spaces and innovative recreation areas, all with a careful emphasis on social, environmental and economic sustainability.
